Sugar Cookies

Butter 1 stick
Veggie Oil 3/8 cup
Sugar 2/3 cup
Powdered Sugar 1/4 cup (try more)
Vanilla Extract 1 T
Eggs 1 egg
Flour 2 cups
Baking Soda 1/4 t
Cream of Tartar 1/4 t
Salt 1 dash
YIELDS: ~7 big-ish cookies

Combine everything except the flower. Beat it to death. Gently mix in the flower. Cook until the edges just begin to golden, or until the dough spot on the bottom is what you what it to be. For a convection oven, that usually means bake at 200 degrees for 10 minutes.

Do almond extract instead of vanilla for almond cookies (or add both). Pair the almond cookies with a raspberry frosting for happiness. Raspberry frosting can be made by adding raspberry jam to regular vanilla frosting.
