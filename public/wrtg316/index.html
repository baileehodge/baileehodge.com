Introduction

The Meeting Owl 3 is a device that is designed to make business video calls an immersive experience. It acts as a microphone, camera, and speaker. Included with the Meeting Owl 3 is a power supply, AC/DC Cables, a 6.5 ft (2m) USB-C cable, and a quick-start guide for easy setup [see figure 1].

Dimensions: 4.4” W x 4.4” D x 10.72” H (111mm W x 111mm D x 272mm H)

Weight: 2.61lb (1.2kg)

Purpose of this Article

This document was prepared for the use of BYU Continuing Education Employees. Some details of use and procedure are specific to this organization. Most other information in this document is general to Meeting Owl 3 devices.

Safety Tips

The Meeting Owl 3 is a costly product containing complicated and delicate technology. In order to keep these devices functioning for all HCEB employees to use, here are some tips for keeping the devices safe:

  • Keep food and drink at a safe distance from the device.
  • Place the device on a table or other secure elevated space for use. Never place the device on the floor.
  • Only use the device indoors.

Camera

Unique Qualities of The OwlLabs Camera

The Meeting Owl 3 has a panoramic fisheye camera with a 360° field of view. This allows it to show all participants of a meeting when placed in the center of a table. The device has software that tracks who is speaking and focuses the camera on that person. The device simultaneously streams 360° footage. These features are designed with hybrid work environments in mind. The speaker tracking software helps viewers who are participating remotely to feel more present in the meeting.

Specifications

Camera hardware specs:

  • Output Resolution: 1080p HD

  • Field of View: 360°

  • Video Pickup Radius: 10 ft (3m)

  • Pair two Meeting Owls to expand video and audio range by 8ft (2.5m) in any direction

Microphones

Unique Qualities of The OwlLabs Microphones

The Meeting Owl 3 has eight built-in microphones surrounding the perimeter of the device. These microphones are beamforming (meaning the microphones are configured to each capture sound from a specific direction) to produce more immersive sound. The microphones are also classified as Smart Mics, which means that they equalize audio input to amplify more soft-spoken voices. This is especially beneficial in the HCEB conference rooms on the fourth floor; the long tables in these rooms might put some in-person participants farther away from the Meeting Owl device than others.

Specifications

Microphone hardware specs:

  • Eight interconnected omni-directional beamforming Smart Mics

  • Audio Pickup Radius: 18ft (5.5m)

  • Pair two Meeting Owls to expand video and audio range by 8ft (2.5m) in any direction

Audio Output

Qualities

The Meeting Owl 3 contains three built-in speakers. The multi-speaker setup is intended to provide clear sound for in-person participants. The device can also pair to external speakers through wired USB-C or micro-hdmi connections, as well as bluetooth connections. The Meeting Owl 3 cannot connect to the Jabra speakers that come standard in the HCEB conference rooms.

Specifications

Speaker hardware specs

  • Three built-in speakers

  • 360° sound coverage

  • Speaker Output Level: 80 db SPL

Connection Capabilities

Software Connection

The Meeting Owl 3 integrates with both Microsoft Teams and Zoom, which are standard use for employees of the CE Department. The device also connect with the following communication software: BlueJeans, GoToMeeting, webex, Google Meet, and Slack.

Hardware Connection

The Meeting Owl 3 can be connected to the conference room computers via wired or wireless connection. The devices cannot be connected to the in-room camera, not can it be connected to the room's designated Jabra speaker. The Meeting Owl device provides higher audio and video quality than these devices, anyway.
